About

I began this migration to a full stack designer from a background in graphic design.

I like small snippets of things that by themselves arent much, but when all the pieces are put together, you have something spectacular. Thats why I Iike quilting, stained glass, and yes - coding.

Its not only aesthetically a work of art, but serves a function as well. I think that makes the world a more enjoyable place to live.

Through my years of Graphic Design, I have become proficient in Photoshop which has been an asset to my projects. It just adds to the fun!
